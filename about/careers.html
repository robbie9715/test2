Our goal is to make online education work for everyone.

We don’t have all the answers today, but we believe that by assembling a team that comes from different professional, personal, and educational backgrounds, together we will figure it out.

Our team is currently on several continents and numerous time zones. Between us, we have done hundreds of online courses and some online degrees. We don’t care if you have a degree; we care if you know how to learn. We are similarly flexible about where, when, and how you work. Class Central is a remote-first, asynchronous workplace.

Company

Class Central is a listing of online courses . We aggregate courses from many providers to make it easy to find the best courses on almost any subject, wherever they exist. We focus primarily on free (or free to audit) courses from universities, offered through massive open online course (MOOC) platforms. Whatever you are interested in learning, it is more than likely that our catalog includes a course that will meet your needs.

Our stats and analysis have been quoted in the New York Times, the Philadelphia Inquirer, the Chronicle of Higher Education, Forbes, and the Chicago Tribune. Our articles about online learning regularly go viral on Quartz and Medium. Class Central's founder has written for EdSurge, TechCrunch, Venturebeat, Fast Company, and Observer.
